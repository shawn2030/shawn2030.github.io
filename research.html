Research

Improving Variational Autoencoders (VAEs)

Explored techniques to enhance the performance of VAEs, focusing on improving generative modeling by incorporating methods such as Importance Weighted Autoencoders (IWAE) and Beta-VAE. This work bridges the gap between theoretical understanding and practical implementations of VAEs in diverse domains.

Advancing Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)

Researched advanced GAN techniques including Wasserstein GANs, Conditional GANs, and Laplacian Pyramid GANs for generating high-resolution and high-fidelity images. Addressed challenges such as mode collapse and artifacts in generative outputs.

AI in Sports Analytics

Developed innovative applications of AI in sports analytics, including a system for offside detection using a single camera. This research focuses on leveraging AI to solve real-world challenges efficiently, reducing hardware dependencies.
